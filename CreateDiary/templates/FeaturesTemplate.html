Features

S-Diary comes with a set of some powerful features that help you to write securely, your personalized diary with text only(image-testing is in testing phase). Each S-Diary account comes with a set of basic login system, add diaries based on dates and also a profile facility.

Make your memories count

"

Life is not measured by the number of breathes we take but by the number of moments that take our breath away

"

We believe that your moments are precious and cannot be evaluated by a mere number of likes and comments. So, keep an account of your best days that matter most.

Distraction-free design

We build a simple, plain and effective User interface so as to ensure a distraction-free environment suitable for writing diary,

Write wherever you go

S-Diary is reponsive on almost all devices so you can write at any moment with any device you have making your transition from smartphones to desktop ,or vice-versa smooth.
